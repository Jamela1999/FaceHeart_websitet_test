Affiliate terms & conditions

Welcome to the Nexsas Affiliate Program!
Please read these terms carefully to ensure you fully understand how our program works and what is expected.

How does it work?

As a Nexsas affiliate, you are part of our extended sales team.
For every customer you refer who completes a purchase, you'll earn a commission. The more you refer, the more you can earn there's no limit!

How will I know if I made a sale?

You'll receive a notification when a referred customer makes a purchase. You can also track your sales and commissions in real-time from your affiliate dashboard.

How do I get paid?

  • All payments are processed via PayPal.
  • Ensure your PayPal account information is correctly set in your affiliate profile.

When are payouts made?

  • Payouts are made at the end of each month.
  • Minimum payout threshold: $100.
  • Commissions must be verified manually before release.
  • For example, commissions earned in May will be paid by June 30.

Cookie duration

When a visitor clicks your link, a 30-day cookie is set. If they complete a purchase within that period, you receive the commission unless another affiliate's link overwrites yours.
